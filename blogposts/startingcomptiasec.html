Starting My CompTIA Security+ Studies

- July 24, 2023

I'm excited to share that I'm starting to study for my CompTIA Security+ certification. As a student of cybersecurity, I am always looking for ways to enhance my knowledge and skills in the field. I'm particularly interested in the practical skills that Security+ provides, as it is an entry-level certification that covers a wide range of security topics, including network security, cryptography, and identity management.

Studying for Security+ is also a personal project for me, as I'm looking forward to learning on my own schedule and without the pressure of deadlines. It's a refreshing change of pace compared to the demands of university coursework and CTFs. I hope that by earning this certification, I'll gain a better understanding of the cybersecurity field and identify areas of interest that I'd like to explore further.

My goal is to study diligently throughout the Summer & Fall and aim to take the exam in mid-December. I'm looking forward to sharing updates on my progress and insights gained along the way.
